Rabbi Schechter is a Musmach of Yeshiva Torah VoDaath having received a Yoreh Yoreh Smicha in 1985 and a Yadin Yadin Smicha in 1988, both from HaRav Avraham Pam, ZT"L. He also received a Masters Degree in Computer Science from NYU - Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences in 1985. Since leaving Kollel in 1989 Rabbi Schechter & Ruchie have served pulpits in Oceanside and Merrick on Long Island and in Fair Lawn, New Jersey before taking over the Rabbinic Leadership of Congregation Ohr Moshe in 2001. Rabbi Schechter served as a member of Agudas HaRabonim and as a member and officer of the Young Israel Council of Rabbis. Currently Rabbi Schechter serves as a member of the Vaad HaChinuch and as the 12th grade Halacha instructor at Shevach High School for Girls (where he won the Gruss Award for Excellence in Teaching in 2002) and he serves as the Rav HaMachshir at the Bagel Boss chain and several other establishments in, Queens, Brooklyn, Long Island & NYC. Ruchie and the Rabbi also run from their home office a small computer software and service company specializing in Estate, Trust & Guardianship Court Accountings.

Rabbi Schechter's grandfather was HaGaon HaRav Eliezer Zev Kirzner ZT"L of Bnei Yehuda Shul in Boro Park, a world renowned Talmid Chacham who instilled within him the love for Rabbanus. Ruchie's father is Rabbi Peretz Steinberg, Shlita of Young Israel of Queens Valley, under whose tutelage and in whose Shul the Schechters gained much valuable experience.

Ruchie and the Rabbi are blessed with 6 children ranging in ages from 13 to 29, a daughter-in-law, 3 sons-in-law and 6 grandchildren.
